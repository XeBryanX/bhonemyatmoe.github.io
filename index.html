Bio-Wikipedia

Bhone Myat Moe (Burmese; 12 November 2009) is a person known for his intelligence and humor. He was born in Htoo Myat Hospital in Monywa.

Family

He had 3 aunts, 1 uncle, 2 cousins and an older brother. He unfortunately lost his aunt and one of his cousins in the 2008 Nargis cyclone. Grandfather from his mother side had already passed away before he was born and ever since then, he only had 3 grandparents. Around the age of 3 is when he lost is other grandfather leaving him with only his grandmothers. Later in his life around the age of 11, He lost his grandmother from his father side due to covid. He currently only have his grandmother from his mother side.

Academic History

In his early years, He attended Teacher Susan Preschool (Monywa) for kindergarten. He later attended B.A.E (Monywa) for his 1st Grade. He switched schools to Panntagon ( Monywa ) for his 2nd and 3rd year which was a very traumatic school for him. He suffered massive amount of bullying, abuse and mental strain for a kid who was only 9. Due to these reasons he later moved to Global Smart ( Monywa ) for his 4th, 5th and 6th years which were one of the best years of his life. He met so many great friends and people he can trust. Due to reasons concerning his safety, His parents sent him to Mandalay to study while living with his aunt and the only remaining grandparent. He is currently attending NVL University ( Mandalay ) for his Pre-IGCSE and IGCSE courses.

Romance

All his life, he was interested in a few different people that he liked romantically but none seemed to like him back. Partially because he never confess to the people he like. There was a case with a girl by the name of Bella. who had the same feelings for him but they didn't know that they liked each other which nothing happened between them. Later he realized she had feelings for him only for him to not feel the same anymore.

Talents and Skills

Bryan wasn't a person who was highly talented but he did have some skills that he was rather proud of. He enjoys drawing and some of his drawings are decent looking. He learnt to play the piano ever since he was a little kid and ever since then, he has been playing the piano. He can speak 2 languages fluently, his native language Burmese and English.
